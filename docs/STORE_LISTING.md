## Full description
Terra is not a video loop. It is a live simulation of Earth, computed on your
phone, every second, from real data.

THE PLANET — REAL-TIME EARTH
• The globe rotates at Earth's true speed, driven by your clock — one turn per
  sidereal day, always facing exactly the way Earth faces right now
• Sunlight falls where it truly falls: the day/night line on your wallpaper
  matches the real one, minute by minute, season by season
• Real cloud cover from EUMETSAT weather satellites, refreshed every 3 hours —
  the storm on the news is the storm on your screen
• City lights trace the night side
• The Moon rides its true orbit, at true scale, with the correct phase
• Behind it, the real night sky: the Hipparcos star catalogue, the Milky Way,
  and the five naked-eye planets at their actual positions
• NASA-derived 8K imagery with terrain relief lighting

ACCURATE. LIVE. ADAPTING.
Nothing is prerecorded. Sunrise happens when your sunrise happens. Tonight's
stars are tonight's stars. Come back in an hour and the planet has turned.

PREMIUM, BY DESIGN
• No ads. No subscriptions. No accounts. No tracking. Ever.
• One-time purchase — new worlds added over time
• Battery-aware: rendering fully pauses whenever the wallpaper is hidden
• Works offline with built-in imagery

Uninstall and it is gone completely — no leftovers, no background services.
