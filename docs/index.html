TERRA LIVE WALLPAPERS
Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 8, 2026

The short version

Terra Live Wallpapers collects no personal data. None. The app has no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking of any kind.

What the app does

Terra renders a real-time view of Earth as your device's live wallpaper. All rendering happens locally on your device.

Network access

The app uses your internet connection only to download publicly available imagery: NASA-derived Earth textures from public content delivery networks, and near-real-time cloud cover maps derived from EUMETSAT satellite data (clouds.matteason.co.uk). These are plain image downloads; no personal information, identifiers, or usage data are sent. Without a connection the wallpaper continues to work with built-in visuals.

Data collection, sharing and retention

We do not collect, store, share, or sell any user data. There is nothing to retain and nothing to delete.

Permissions

The app requests only the INTERNET permission, for the imagery downloads described above.

Children

The app is suitable for all ages and collects no data from anyone, including children.
